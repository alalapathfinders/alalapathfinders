About Al-Ala Pathfinders

A student-led initiative nurturing faith, character, and leadership through meaningful learning experiences.

Our Story

Al-Ala Pathfinders began quietly.

It started with a few conversations, a shared concern, and guidance that trusted young hearts with responsibility. No announcements. No noise.

A name was chosen, not to claim leadership, but to describe a journey.

Since then, the work has continued.
Sometimes visible. Often unseen.

Students keep stepping forward, learning, serving, and growing in turn.

This is Al-Ala Pathfinders:
a cycle of trust, guidance, and continuity,
where leadership passes on, but values remain.

Our Purpose & Values

Purpose

To follow the straight path and to lead others to it.

Guidance

Walking with clarity, sincerity, and direction.

Responsibility

Trusting youth with leadership and accountability.

Continuity

Passing values forward, generation by generation.
